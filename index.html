About Us



Mission


We are Marketing Pioneers, your digital marketers on demand. Founded in 2020 in Fairfield, Connecticut by Sacred Heart University’s Jack Welch College of Business to solve a problem all small businesses face and can no longer ignore digital marketing. Our team is made up of more than 50 graduate students pursuing their Master’s in Digital Marketing, equipped with the mindset, tools, techniques, and training to optimize your business and revolutionize your customer’s experience online.

Vision


At Marketing Pioneers, we bridge the gap between emerging talent, powerful institutions, and businesses within the community. The result? A powerful professional network that sets up our community with the framework needed to succeed in a digital world.


Our Core Values


Innovation


We go beyond traditional approaches, adventure and challenges are the heart of our vision. We drive our emerging talent out of their comfort zone where the innovative ideas can happen. The spirit of innovation and outstanding performance is our key to development. We offer creative ideas, unique, and innovative solutions. We have professional competencies who are seeing things from multiple perspectives.

Intergrity


We earn your trust. We speak honestly and act ethically, and our personality guides our daily work. We win other’s trust by doing the right things, the right way. Based on our integrity and honesty, we do everything needed to create value for all of our stakeholders. We believe that commitment and demonstration of honesty and moral behavior in an academic scope leads us to success. In this process, we are responsible for our own actions and decisions.

Collaboration


Our DM program is in a great position to help bridge the gap between education and the business community. Through effective engagement and collaboration between businesses and students, we support business and economic growth, we develop community relations, and we grant opportunity to support student employment.
